About Us

Welcome To WAKA Ltd

At WAKA Ltd, we are committed to excellence in the real estate sector, delivering end-to-end property solutions for individuals, businesses, and investors. Our expertise spans across property sales, acquisitions, management, and consulting, ensuring our clients benefit from a truly comprehensive service. With a dedicated team of experienced professionals, we combine in-depth market knowledge with a client-first approach to create tailored solutions that address the unique needs of each customer. We pride ourselves on building lasting relationships founded on trust, transparency, and results. From sourcing prime properties to managing furnished accommodations such as our Waka Hotel in Zanzibar, we bring innovation, efficiency, and a deep understanding of market dynamics to every project we handle.

Our Mission

To provide unmatched real estate services that drive the growth, satisfaction, and prosperity of our clients, while contributing positively to the communities we serve.
