About US

Welcome to Waka Your Home Away from Home in Zanzibar

Welcome to Waka – Your Home Away from Home in Zanzibar Just minutes from Zanzibar International Airport, Waka offers the perfect blend of comfort, style, and convenience. Whether you’re here for a quick stopover, a business trip, or a relaxing holiday, our furnished hotel provides everything you need to feel at home – and more. Why Stay at Waka Unbeatable Location: Skip the long transfers. Arrive, relax, and be at your room in minutes after landing. Fully Furnished Comfort: Stylish rooms designed for both short and long stays, complete with modern amenities to make your stay stress-free. Breathtaking Rooftop Dining: Enjoy delicious local and international cuisine at our rooftop restaurant while taking in sweeping views of Zanzibar’s coastline. Perfect for a romantic dinner or unwinding after a busy day. Personalized Service: Our team goes above and beyond to ensure your experience is warm, welcoming, and memorable.
